About Me

Hello! I'm Thiago Cunha, a Brazilian Data Architect and Team Leader currently based in Wrocław, Poland. I have over a decade of experience in Business Intelligence, specializing in QlikView and Qlik Sense development, data modeling, and leading BI teams to success.

My journey began as a system analyst and developer, and throughout the years I've grown into leadership roles, mentoring new developers and designing data architectures that make a real impact. I love turning complex data into clear, actionable insights that drive results.

Outside of work, I'm a proud geek with a passion for anime (yes, Gurren Lagann is a favorite), storytelling, and anything tech-related. I'm also a husband and father, navigating life abroad with my family. I'm deeply committed to continuous learning, empathy in leadership, and building systems that are not only efficient — but human-friendly.

If you're curious about data, BI, or geek culture, we already have something in common. Let’s connect!
